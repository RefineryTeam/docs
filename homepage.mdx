## What is Refinery?

Refinery is a development team focused on creating high-quality software for gaming communities and developers.

Our work includes:

- **Minecraft Plugins** built for Paper, Spigot, and compatible server software.
- **Discord Bots** with modern slash commands, automation, and integrations.
- **Server Systems & Integrations** tailored for individual communities.
- **Open-Source Libraries & Resources** for developers.
- **Custom Development Services** for communities requiring unique functionality.

Whether you're running a small community server or a large network, Refinery aims to provide software that is reliable, configurable, and easy to manage.
